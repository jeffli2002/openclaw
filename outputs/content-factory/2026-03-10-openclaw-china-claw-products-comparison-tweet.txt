Tested all 7 major Claw products in China: OpenClaw, MaxClaw, Kimi Claw, CoPaw, QClaw, WorkBuddy, ArkClaw.

Key findings:
- ArkClaw: fastest setup (30 sec), best for "just work"
- OpenClaw + CoPaw: most powerful, best for developers
- WorkBuddy: enterprise WeChat integration, best for teams
- Kimi Claw: best for long document analysis

No single "best" product - choose based on your use case.
